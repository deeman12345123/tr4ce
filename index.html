Our Story

A passion for traditional confectionery

Tr4ces Treatz is a family-run pop-up market stall bringing the charm of old-fashioned lolly shops to Western Victoria. We believe in the simple pleasure of traditional confectionery and the joy it brings to people of all ages.

Our stall features carefully selected classic lollies, nostalgic treats, and premium chocolates. We specialize in tracking down rare and hard-to-find sweets ‚Äî from discontinued Aussie favourites to exotic international imports you won't find in regular shops!

Whether you're reliving childhood memories, discovering new flavours from around the world, or hunting for that special lolly you thought was gone forever, we're here to make every visit special. Our selection changes weekly with new rare finds!

We take pride in our friendly service, quality products, and the warm, welcoming atmosphere of our traditional market stall. Visit us at Horsham Market and experience the sweetness of simpler times!
